Belgian Beerfest 2024


The Annual Belgian Beerfest Craft Brewing Competition will be held from 9am on Saturday 25th Ma y2024, at The Woori Community House,1385 Healesville-Koo Wee Rup Rod, Woori Yallock Victoria. Melways Ref: 286F11

Entries may be submitted upto midday, Saturday 6th April 2024 at the following collection points:

  • The Full Brew: Shop 4/ 1389 Healesville-Kooweerup Rd Woori Yallock Ph:5964 6222
  • The Brewer’s Den: 253 Dorset Road Boronia Ph:9761 1900
  • Grain & Grape: 5/ 280 Whitehall St Yarraville Ph: 9687 0061
  • Keg King: 2/33-35 Smith Road Springvale Ph: 9011 1698

NOTE: Etries will not be accepted at the event as in previous years
