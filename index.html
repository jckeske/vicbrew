What Vicbrew does

Vicbrew exists to strengthen homebrewing in Victoria. The committee brings clubs together, supports competition organisers, and helps maintain a clear pathway from local events through to the Australian Amateur Brewing Championships.

  • Run the Victorian state championships

    Vicbrew organises the annual Victorian Amateur Brewing Championships and keeps the event visible to brewers, judges, and stewards across the state.

  • Support clubs and open competitions

    The committee promotes inter-club activity, shares information between organisers, and helps make the competition calendar easier to navigate.

  • Improve judging and style consistency

    Vicbrew encourages better judging standards, consistent use of style guidelines, and ongoing education for judges and stewards.
