Australian Amateur Brewing Championships 2006

The Australian Amateur Brewing Championships will be held at the at Bell's Hotel Brewery, Corner Moray St. and Coventry St. South Melbourne on Sunday 12th November from 9:00am.

Entry is via qualification through the respective State championships.
