Victorian Amateur Brewing Championships 2008

The Victorian Amateur Brewing Championships (Vicbrew 2008) will be held on 4th & 5th October 2008 at Beer DeLuxe, Federation Square, Flinders Street Melbourne. Melways reference 1A P10.

Placing (1st, 2nd or 3rd) in any category at Vicbrew 2008 qualifies entrant to submit an entry, in the same category, at AABC 2008. Winner of the Vicrew "Champion Brewer” award will recieve a free "all sessions pass" for the Australian National HomeBrew Conference courtesy of the ANHC committee
