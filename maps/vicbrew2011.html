Victorian Amateur Brewing Championships 2011

The Victorian Amateur Brewing Championships (Vicbrew 2011) will be held on 24th & 25th September 2011 at the Belgian Beer Café Eureka, 5 Riverside Quay, Southbank Melbourne. Melways reference: 2F E7 .

Placing (1st, 2nd or 3rd) in any category at Vicbrew 2011 qualifies entrant to submit an entry, in the same category, at AABC 2011 Adelaide.

Online Entries:

Online Entry Registration is available for the first time at Vicbrew 2011 via compmaster http://www.compmaster.com.au
NOTE: Online entries will still need to be delivered to a Vicbrew 2011 collection centre by Saturday 10/09/2011

 

Entries may be submitted upto Saturday 10th September 2011 at the following collection points:
