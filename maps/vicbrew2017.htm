Victorian Amateur Brewing Championships 2017

The Victorian Amateur Brewing Championships (Vicbrew 2017) will be held on 16th & 17th September 2017. Venue is yet to be confirmed.

Please note the revised categories and sub-styles for 2017.

Placing (1st, 2nd or 3rd) in any category at Vicbrew 2017 qualifies entrant to submit an entry, in the same category, at AABC 2017 Western Australia.


 

Online Entries:

Online Entry Registration is available for Vicbrew 2017 From August 1st via compmaster http://www.compmaster.com.au
NOTE: Online entries will still need to be delivered to a Vicbrew 2017 collection centre by Early Saturday 26/08/2017
