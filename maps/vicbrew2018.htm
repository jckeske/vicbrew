Victorian Amateur Brewing Championships 2018

The Victorian Amateur Brewing Championships (Vicbrew 2018) will be held on 22nd & 23rd September 2018 at the Belgian Beer Café Eureka, 5 Riverside Quay, Southbank Melbourne. Melways reference: 2F E7 .
Please note that VicBrew uses the AABC2018 categories and styles, which are the same as in 2017. 2018 Categories and Styles listing.

Placing (1st, 2nd or 3rd) in any category at Vicbrew 2018 qualifies entrant to submit an entry, in the same category, at AABC 2018 Melbourne Australia.

 

Online Entries:

Online Entry Registration is available for Vicbrew 2018 From July 14th via compmaster http://www.compmaster.com.au
Please follow the competition rules on the Compmaster page.
NOTE: Online entries will still need to be delivered to a Vicbrew 2018 collection centre by Early Saturday 01/09/2018
