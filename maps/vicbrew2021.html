Victorian Amateur Brewing Championships 2021

The Victorian Amateur Brewing Championships (Vicbrew 2021) will be held on 27th & 28th November 2021 at the Belgian Beer Café Eureka, 5 Riverside Quay, Southbank Melbourne. Melways reference: 2F E7 .

Due to COVID and forced delay of the competition Vicbrew 2021 will be a State Championship only with no qualifying for AABC 2021.
