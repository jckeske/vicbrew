Victorian Amateur Brewing Championships 2022

The Victorian Amateur Brewing Championships (Vicbrew 2022) will be held on 10th & 11th September 2022 at the Belgian Beer Café Eureka, 5 Riverside Quay, Southbank Melbourne. Melways reference: 2F E7 .

Please note the new categories and styles list for 2022.

Placing (1st, 2nd or 3rd) in any category at Vicbrew 2022 qualifies entrant to submit an entry, in the same category, at AABC 2022 Melbourne Australia on October 13th 2022.

Online Entry Registration is available for Vicbrew 2022 From June 1st via http://comps.vicbrew.org/
