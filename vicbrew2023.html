Victorian Amateur Brewing Championships 2023

The Victorian Amateur Brewing Championships (Vicbrew 2023) will be held on 9th & 10th September 2023 at the Belgian Beer Café Eureka, 5 Riverside Quay, Southbank Melbourne. Melways reference: 2F E7 .

Please note there has been one change for 2023. New Zealand Pils has been moved from Pale Ale is in the Pale Lager Category for 2023

2023 Categories and styles list

Placing (1st, 2nd or 3rd) in any category at Vicbrew 2023 qualifies entrant to submit an entry, in the same category, at AABC 2023 Adelaide on October 21st 2023.

Online Entries:

Online Entry Registration is available for Vicbrew 2023 From June 1st via http://comps.vicbrew.org/

NOTE: Online entries will still need to be delivered to a Vicbrew 2023 collection centre by Early Saturday 19/08/2023
